My promises

I hereby promise to love every second used to plan, to chat away and execute your vision, to hold it dearly and follow it for better and for worse.

I promise to always talk with grace, answer every email, calm every doubt, straighten up every naughty napkin.

I will earnestly bring the best design from within you. Guide you in being yourselves through and through.

I promise to spend with you all the time you need, to share in your joy and bring some of my own, to give you my full attention and strengthen your imagination and creativity.

Be sure of my kindness, immeasurable passion for weddings, and my dedication to yours.

I pledge to bargain for you. To get you any possible discount, regardless of how much I dislike asking for handouts. But I will do it. For you. You deserve not only the best service but the best cooperation and support too.

I promise to be your rock in case of unmatched chairs, or venue cancellation. To bring peace and fairy lights during the long months of planning and deciding.

To work with patience and to be willing to face all the changes, revisions, and guest additions.

Though I will share all my Pinterest boards with you, I will gently draw you back to your budget limits, making sure you respect it.

I give you my word, I won't desert you in case of Bridezilla features arising in you, I will even knit socks for you in case cold feet.

I will faithfully serve the richer and poorer, and no matter what, I will count your benefit higher than my fee.

I grant you my skills and the best my abilities.

My friendship shall envelop our working relationship and I will strive to be a blessing.

I value your quirks and your traits, your passions and your hobbies, I plan to make them shine on your big day, allowing them to shape, color and make it memorable.

The values and principles described above are based on Paul's letter to the Galatians chapter 5, verse 22 from the New Testament.
